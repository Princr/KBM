Introduction

We are interested in building businesses, social enterprises and personal brands that are uniquely African. Because we believe a unique brand is a global brand. Our goal is to help businesses and their brands realize their fullest potential by offering expert brand management advisory services and shaping innovations in the field of marketing and public relations that will help springboard our clients and their brands into global competitors.

Pure Creativity.

About Us

Founded purely in the spirit of entrepreneurship by serial entrepreneur and founding CEO, Ntando Ndlovu, Kingstone Brand Management Agency (KBM) came into existence in the year 2015 to provide an advisory springboard for both start-up and established brands in Africa. We are a team of brand management specialists with a keen interest in creating and growing brands through our expert integration of online and social media activity, blended with unique on-the-ground field activities. Our team of innovators offers a synergy of talent that allows us to present unique solutions to wide ranging strategic issues faced by our clients. Our focus and expertise encompasses Corporate brands, Sports brand management, and Entertainment and Lifestyle brands. Political personalities, Business

leaders, Church leaders and other public figures with a need for expert reputation and public relations management make up the remainder of our client portfolio.


A brand stretches far beyond the product or service a business offers; it encompasses company culture, service delivery, perception and expectation. Inspired by boxless thinking, at KBM Agency we have a team of innovators who know how to perfectly mix all these moving parts that eventually make up your brand. We not only solve problems holding back your brand, but we will also help identify opportunities for further expansion and brand growth.
